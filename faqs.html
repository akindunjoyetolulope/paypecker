    General

    What is Gloopro?

    Gloopro is a powerful, cost-effective, and secure cloud-based enterprise eProcurement and commerce platform. It is designed to help large and medium enterprises streamline purchasing processes, control costs and manage vendors related to material and service inputs not included in their core products/services (aka, indirect spend). With Gloopro, you can electronically collaborate within your organization and your procurement department to enhance your purchasing workflow. You can use Gloopro to develop a standardised procurement process, which you can enable company-wide to save money across all departments.

    How is Gloopro different from other supplier and purchasing management platforms?

    The Gloopro platform was designed to revolutionalize the technological landscape of corporate purchasing. Large and middle-market enterprises in a wide variety of industry verticals are able to manage their indirect spend with ease and efficiency.

    We are continually evolving the platform to ensure our customers can control their external and internal procurement objectives and get ideal prices and terms from the best supplier sources, whilst focusing on automating the process, thereby saving our customers money, time and attention.

    Information security is my priority. Can I trust this application?

    The safety of your data is critically important to us. We use EV SSL to encrypt your confidential data. That means all information exchanged between you, your suppliers and Gloopro are fully protected. In addition, our multi-user and multi-level architecture provides an unlimited number of users their own private purchasing access, keeping user content confidential and secure.

    Which industries can benefit from this software?

    Gloopro provides workflow advantages and savings for large and mid-market enterprises in all industries. Contact us to get more information specific to your industry.

    Others

    How do I get started?

    Complete the company registration form. We will complete your account creation one business day after receiving your completed registration.

    Who can register a company to use Gloopro?

    Anyone authorized to manage an organization or its purchases can register. We do require that you use a business email for account authenticity.

    Who is the account administrator?

    You decide: the administrator may be the person who registers the company, or another person the company authorizes as administrator. You are also able to assign any number of users as administrators.

    What can the administrator do?

    The administrator can set user permissions and other controls. The administrator can also add users, oversee the work of these users, add branches, and authorize branch managers to set user permissions.

    How many users can work on a single user account?

    User accounts are designed to be accessed by one user only. Each individual using Gloopro should have a unique username and password for purposes of security and optimized workflow.
