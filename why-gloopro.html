    • Why Gloopro?

    A rare combination of disparate organizational capabilities into a seamless, all-in-one-place eProcurement solution

    Supply-Chain Depth

    Over 6 years deep vertical relationships and strategic partnerships in the office consumables, office essentials, office equipment and industrial equipment supply chains in Nigeria, Europe and China, across several industry verticals.

    Logistics Excellence

    Proprietary and partnership-based logistics value chain structure designed and built from ground up, tailored to the complex needs of large and medium enterprises across many industry verticals, with full national coverage across the 36 states of Nigeria and the FCT.

    Scale Economics

    Vertical economics of scale provides capacity to offer competitive prices across all the product and service categories in our multi-enterprise supply chain marketplace.

    Trust Layer

    Unlike in developed economies, the supply chain in Africa is mostly not well structured. We act as your layer of trust in your interactions with suppliers, bringing structure, transparency, guaranteed service levels and product quality to these, with service recovery systems to mitigate the risks from suppliers.

    Technology Leadership

    World-renowned, best-in-class technology and innovation leadership in the African startup ecosystem, featured on CNN, BBC, Forbes, The Next Web, Financial Times, Economist and Washington Post. Legacy brand voted 4th on Mashable's list of 20 African Startups to Watch--1st in Nigeria.
