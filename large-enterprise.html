    The Problem

    Indirect spend procurement for multinationals and large enterprises (with over $200million revenue) is expensive, fragmented and time consuming. Every year, large enterprises:

    • Dedicate more than 43,000 man-hours to the procurement of various categories of products and services with which to run their businesses and serve their customers.
    • Work with hundreds of category suppliers in a complex mesh of slow, inefficient, failure-prone legacy approaches for fulfilling their procurement requirements, which are therefore fraught with delays and disappointments.
    • Spend up to $53million directly on the recurrent material and capital inputs into their yearly operations, with as close as 5% of these being lost to inefficiencies, shenanigans, "padding," and outright fraud.

    The Solution

    A One-Stop platform to simplify, automate and make procurement more efficient and transparent for large enterprises and multinationals. Gloopro Integra:

    • Connects all vendors, products and delivery logistics in one simple, seamless interface, minimising the human resource components required to execute the procurement processes of large enterprises.
    • Streamline interactions with suppliers and partners into a process flow that is easily available organization-wide, responsive, auditable, events-driven and performance-based.
    • Drive costs down by cutting down man-hours required for the procurement function, preventing shenanigans and deterring fraud. As much as 14% saved from legacy procurement costs and up to 23% reduction in cycle time from requisition to delivery.
